WHY INVEST


Discover why the United States is the top destination for business investment.

Success in the U.S. market can help drive success globally. Our nation is incredibly diverse, with the world’s most attractive consumer market, a thriving culture of innovation, and a highly productive workforce.

The United States is also home to a wealth of natural resources and a fair, transparent business environment. Here, companies of all sizes—from startups to multinationals—can find the ideas, resources, and markets they need to be competitive.

A Land of Resources

Find the diverse, abundant resources you need for your business to be successful globally. Diverse, abundant natural resources linked by strong infrastructure Third largest land area in the world, varied geography, several climate zones

Energy
  • Low-cost, stable energy supply
  • Leader in sustainable energy technology—see current Energy industry page (needs updating)

Infrastructure
  • Ports, highways, airports
  • Poised for new investment for further expansion and modernization

Global Markets

When you do business in the United States, you have access to over 760 million consumers . Our lucrative and open consumer market is connected with 20 other international markets through free trade agreements.

The Trans-Pacific Partnership (TPP) would increase the access of U.S.-based companies to over one billion consumers.

  • The agreement builds upon existing agreements with:
  • Australia, Canada, Chile, and Mexico
  • while forging new trade relationships with Brunei
  • Japan, Malaysia, New Zealand, and Vietnam
