About us

Helping American Companies Succeed Globally Export.gov brings together resources from across the U.S. Government to assist in the development and growth of American businesses in the international market. We work in partnership with U.S. companies to develop an export plan, implement their international sales strategies, and succeed in today's global marketplace.

Why export?

CLIENTS

Trade Events

The Trade Events are for U.S. businesses interested in selling their products and services overseas. These events include industry conferences, webinars, lectures, and trade missions organized by ITA and other trade agencies including: The U.S. Trade and Development Agency The State Department The Small Business Administration.

Brazil: Green Port Technologies Industry Roundtable for U.S. Companies Los Angeles, California

July 12 , 2015

The U.S. Trade and Development Agency will host an Industry Roundtable in Los Angeles, anticipated to occur during the July 12 22, 2015 timeframe (Date TBD) , where U.S. companies can learn about upcoming opportunities in Brazil and meet with senior-level delegates participating in the Green Port Technologies Reverse Trade Mission.

Basic Guide to Exporting Webinar Series: Pricing, Quotations and Terms

July 22 , 2015

In this webinar, you will learn how to price your product properly, provide complete and accurate quotations, and choose the terms of the sale. In addition, you will also learn about the proper Incoterms for your shipment.
